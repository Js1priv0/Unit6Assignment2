Job Specification

Desired Qualifications:

To be able to work for our company, you will need specific requirements and knowledge, which may include:

  • GCSE in IT (or BTEC equivalent)
  • GCSE in Art (or BTEC equivalent)
  • Level 2 award in Creating a Website
  • Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media
  • Level 2/3 Diploma in Creative iMedia
  • Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology (Specialist)
  • Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills

The essential attributes of the successful Web Developer are a positive attitude and a solid baseline of technical skills. It is critical that the successful candidate is able to demonstrate a genuine passion for digital technology and a true desire to expand their skill-set. Some demonstrable commercial web development experience would be an asset.
